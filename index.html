About Soil & Health Association

We are a charity and incorporated society governed by a volunteer national council of seven elected members. Our small office team manages production of our flagship publication, Organic NZ. We also work with our members and partner organisations to support the development of organic, regenerative, growing. Established in 1941, we are also one of the oldest organisations of our kind in the world.

Volunteer Governance Body

The Soil & Health Association has a strong supporter and membership base. We have a volunteer governance body, a national office with permanent staff members, contract staff, and branches that are run by volunteers. The Association's governance body is called the National Council (NC). It consists of seven members who are appointed by the membership. NC members are elected for a two-year term.
