Support Us

Healthy soils lead to healthy food, healthy people and a healthy planet. Organic regenerative agriculture is a vital tool in building a climate friendly future that protects nature, and our own wellbeing.

Help us help others.

A donation to our 2022 Annual Appeal will support a new generation focused on healthy food production. Our 2022 Annual Appeal is focused on developing our advocacy role and ability to support groups with shared goals but who lack the infrastructure and systems of a mature organisation. Many of these groups are young people, passionate about their local land and determined to feed their families on healthy food.
